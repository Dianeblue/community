The Rolling Kegs Club

Spreading the love of craft for beers sake.

What are the rolling kegs?
We started out as a truck traveling around our home state of Tennessee bringing the local brews of Nashville to smaller towns.

After our first year, it was a success. The demand for quality, smaller batch brews you cant find anywhere else but your home towns brewery was not so much surprising because why not enjoy a beverage that supports your local community? We have expanded into more than 15 states teaming up with their local breweries to help promote the craft.

If you love craft beer, sign up for our mailing list to see where we will be each month. We would love for you to stop by.

Join the rolling kegs club. You will receive special pricing on merchandise, half off drafts any time you find us and early bird ticket purchase. Festival tickets are limited and likely to sell out.

We also cater parties. If you want to impress your guests, we've got you covered. Now taking special requests on your favorite beer. Just so long it's not domestic or imported.

Last but not least Cheers to the craft of beer!
